# koyo.notes
Koyo's notes

This space is focused on **drawing tablets** and how to use them in a way that supports comfort and good working habits.

---

## Overview
Here you will find:
- Information about different types of drawing tablets.
- Guides on how to set up and adjust your tablet.
- Tips on staying comfortable and avoiding strain while drawing.
- Notes and comparisons between tablet models.
- Practical advice for improving your workflow.

---

## Sections
- **Tablet Basics**  
  A simple introduction to what drawing tablets are and how they work.

- **Setup Guides**  
  Easy steps for installing and adjusting your tablet for daily use.

- **Comfort and Ergonomics**  
  Suggestions for posture, desk setup, and healthy drawing habits.

- **Tablet Reviews**  
  Clear notes on different models, with strengths and weaknesses.

- **Helpful Tips**  
  Everyday advice to make drawing smoother and more efficient.

---

## Purpose
The goal of this page is to:
- Share clear and useful information about drawing tablets.  
- Help artists work in a healthier and more comfortable way.  
- Provide practical notes that are easy to follow and apply.
